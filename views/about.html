Overview

WellnessRoom provides a convenient, easy to use, online health care search and appointment booking service. It allows users to scan through different schedules and request appointments with a health care professional at their preferred date and time.

Users

WellnessRoom provides users with a simple method to search and request healthcare appointments from a multitude of providers based on their speciality. It is free, easy to use, convenient and easily accessible on the internet via your PC or any mobile device.

Health Care Professionals

WellnessRoom is a breakthrough for health care professionals. It is a subscription based package that provides online scheduling without requiring clients to switch their scheduling systems. It helps improve practice efficiency and profitability by helping to fill open appointments and last-minute cancellations. WellnessRoom provides patients an alternative online method for booking appointments, freeing up front desk staff from time consuming phone appointments and improving overall customer service and satisfaction.
